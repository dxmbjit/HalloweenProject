Child Tips
  • Cross the street at corners, using traffic signals and crosswalks. Look left, right and left again when crossing and keep looking as you cross.
    Put electronic devices down, keep heads up and walk, don’t run, across the street.
    Teach children to make eye contact with drivers before crossing in front of them.
    Always walk on sidewalks or paths. If there are no sidewalks, walk facing traffic as far to
    the left as possible. Children should walk on direct routes with the fewest street crossings.
Adult Tips
    Only hand out store bought candy
    Dispose of any wrapped candy that has come undone
    Ensure your house’s interior and exterior is well lit
    Create a clear and safe path for trick or treaters
    Do not let trick or treaters into your home
Pet Tips
    Keep pets on a leash (if you are taking them outside)
    Keep pets indoors and away from trick or treaters
    Do not feed your pets Halloween candy
    Don’t dress your pet up unless he/she enjoys it
    Keep decorations and wires out of reach
